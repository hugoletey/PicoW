Exploring Raspberry Pi Pico W and C Language

Introduction to Raspberry Pi Pico W:

Raspberry Pi Pico W is a microcontroller board developed by the Raspberry Pi Foundation, evolving from the original Raspberry Pi Pico version. It features a compact design and harnesses the power of the internal RP2040 microcontroller chip from the Raspberry Pi Foundation. Equipped with built-in wireless capabilities such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, hence the "W" in its name, the Pico W offers extended connectivity, making it ideal for a variety of IoT (Internet of Things) applications.
